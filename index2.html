About Me

Hey, I'm Alvin

I'm a full-stack developer and visual artist from the Philippines, currently studying BS Sports Science at CVSU. I believe the best work happens where logic and creativity meet.

Guided by Aristotle's eudaimonia — I try to live intentionally, build things that matter, and stay curious.

My main interest is understanding the mind — which branches into honing my creativity and design to express, tell stories, and convey ideas effectively.

I'm also drawn to data analysis and pattern recognition in human behavior, as well as understanding the limits and ways to maintain stability in nutrition and individual well-being.
